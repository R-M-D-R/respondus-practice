🛡️ Proctoring Certification Training

🛑 Stop — Read This First

Most students who have problems with tests did NOT follow these instructions.

This training is required before taking any proctored test.

🛡️ Why This Matters

Your tests are monitored using proctoring software.

If your setup does not meet requirements, your attempt will be flagged and invalidated.

✅ What Is Required

  • ✔ Face, head, and ears are clearly visible
  • ✔ Hands and paper visible
  • ✔ Calculator visible (if using a physical one)
  • ✔ Good lighting
  • ✔ A cell phone to act as a second camera
  • ✔ At a table or desk (not a bed, a car, etc.)
  • ✔ Quiet environment with no other people
  • ✔ Professional behavior

🎥 Cameras Setup if you are RIGHT handed

Your cell phone should be set up so that it can see you, your papers where you are working, and your ENTIRE monitor.

✅ Good Setup for a right-handed person. Your cell phone should be placed to YOUR LEFT and angled toward you and your workspace.


This allows the instructor to see your workspace as you work.

Instructor's Viewpoint
You can clearly see the student, their paper, and their ENTIRE monitor.

🎥 Cameras Setup if you are LEFT handed

Your cell phone should be set up so that it can see you, your papers where you are working, and your ENTIRE monitor.

✅ Good Setup for a left-handed person. Your cell phone should be placed to YOUR RIGHT and angled toward you and your workspace.


This allows the instructor to see your workspace as you work.

Instructor's Viewpoint
You can clearly see the student, their paper, and their ENTIRE monitor.

❌ Your work space needs to be visible.

⚠️ Bad Setup - Camera too low

❌ Your face needs to be visible.
